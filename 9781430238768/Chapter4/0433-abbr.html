After the EMP incapacitated all my electronics, I feared I would be trapped on that rooftop without the use of my jetpack. Luckily I still had my trusty Gravity-Assisted Descent Grapple Extension Tool (GADGET) from Power Outfitters. Its double-braided polyfiber construction is purely analog, and thus immune to all electro-magnetic disturbances. I was able to climb down with ease!
